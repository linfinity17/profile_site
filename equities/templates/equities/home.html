Philippine Financial Data

  • PFD is a simple data aggregator / web-scraper that compiles financial information about the Philippine market and provide you with easy analysis tools.
  • Currently, data sources are only for stock prices from the Philippine Stock Exchange.
  • You can easily check historical data for all stocks and compare stock prices relative to each other to see which stocks have performed well over time.
  • This site is a work-in-progress. I hope to bring in more financial data types such as bond prices and government yields in the future.

Stock Comparison

  • In this section, price changes of stocks over a time period are compared side-by-side to see which stock is the best performer.
  • Closing prices are indexed to 100 so you can easily see which how much a stock's price has changed over time.
  • TIP: Include the PSE Index (ticker:PSEI) to see how stocks perform relative to market.

Historical Prices

  • In this section, overall price data for one stock is displayed for a time period.
  • The following price information are displayed:
    • Open
    • High
    • Low
    • Close
    • Volume
